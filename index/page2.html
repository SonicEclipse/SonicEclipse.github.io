Story: While speeding through the Wily Weather Zone, Sonic spotted a raging forest fire. Racing to the scene, he discovered the flames were sparked by Dr. Eggman's latest invention: an organic creature that had gone haywire.

In Sonic Eclipse, our hero must dash across the chaotic Planet Eclipse to defeat Eggman and his creation before the world's natural balance is disrupted.

Join Sonic and his friends as they navigate through lush forests, treacherous mountains, and mysterious caves, all while battling Eggman's robotic minions and the unpredictable creature itself.
